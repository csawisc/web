Background

Comkraft Inc was established in Fairfax, VA in 2003 as an IT Consulting company that focus on advanced technology consulting for the public sector clients. Its founders have extensive experiences in delivery technical solutions to many high-profile clients including DHS, NIH, NASA etc. They own multiple certifications such as ScrumMaster, PMP, IBM Cloud, Microsoft SharePoint, ITILv3 and  have published numerous research papers , technical whitepapers and own multiple granted or pending patents in the area of Artificial Intelligence, Text Mining and have experiences presenting to many  high profile clients. 

 

The current focus area of Comkraft is to provide AI consulting and AI education to demystify AI and infuse AI into enterprise or cloud driven IT solution to make meaningful impact.

 

Comkraft Inc is a proud business partner of IBM Corp and Mentor without Borders Inc.
